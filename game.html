What is Sewer Rat?

Sewer Rat is an text-based adventure game that utilizes survival game aspects like thirst, hunger, and health meters. This turn based is set in a sewer, and expects you, a rat, to venture out on your own. A note waits in front of you, simpily reading 'kill razortooth to stop the oncoming rat war.' Leaving the nest will put you in great peril, as enemies of all sizes try to have a piece of you. Run, fight, die, repeat. Learn the ways of the sewer and you will be a victorious rat.
